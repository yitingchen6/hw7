The Venn diagram provides a high level overview of the Analytics computing platform dependencies.
The platform has to accommodate:
-Data Feeds (some called data pipelines)
-Data Extraction / conversion
-Data mining
-Discovery and Visualization
-Data analytics
-Machine Learning
-Deep Learning
-Archival and long-term retention
-Batch processing: daily and micro batches
Provide access and tools for multiple users:
-Developers
-Data and Business Analysts
-Data Scientists
-End Users
